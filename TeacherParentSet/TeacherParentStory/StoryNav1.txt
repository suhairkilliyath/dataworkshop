The sun had already set by the time Chandran pulled up to his child's school for the parent-teacher conference. He rushed towards the auditorium, cursing the unexpected traffic that delayed him. As he approached the entrance, he could hear muffled discussions emanating from inside. Taking a deep breath, he slowly opened the door, hoping to slip in unnoticed.
The auditorium was bustling with activity. Parents and teachers were huddled in various groups, engaged in animated discussions.
